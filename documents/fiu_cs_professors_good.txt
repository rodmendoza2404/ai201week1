I finished my undergrad a few years ago so the people who teach these courses may have changed a bit.

COP 4610 - Kianoosh Boroojeni

CEN 4010 - Kianoosh Boroojeni

COP 4710 - Sergio Piasno

COP 4104- Gregory Murad Reis

Kianoosh is probably one of the better professors if you apply yourself. He goes into a bunch of detail, he’s passionate about the subject and curves like a mad man.

Gregory Murad Reis is by far my favorite CS professor that I had at FIU. The man is a good teacher and is extremely passionate about the subjects he teaches. He also directly works on robotics projects both with students and industry professionals designed to research and prevent algae blooms in Biscayne bay. I took him for COP 4555 (Principles of Programming Languages) but I saw him teaching COP 4104.

Sergio Pisano is okay, he primarily teaches online courses and thats who i took COP4710 with. No complaints, but the online format sucks.

Antonio Hernandez is also a decent professor though his focus is mainly the mathematics behind algorithms. I took him for Data Structures and Algorithm Techniques. Good professor but his lectures are very math heavy.
